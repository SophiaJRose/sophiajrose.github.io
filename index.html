Hi, my name is Sophia Jennifer Rose (she/her). I am an aspiring software developer with an interest in video game development, and I have made this website as a place to show some of my work, gamedev and otherwise. I am a trans woman, based in the Greater Glasgow region of Scotland. After completing my Master's Degree in Computer Science at the University of Glasgow, I became a professional software developer, and I am currently looking for my next role as such. My professional experience so far is in Java, but I like to use my personal projects to experiment with other languages, such as TypeScript and Ruby, and I am always happy to learn new languages and toolsets. I like to regularly participate in game jams, as an outlet for my interest in game development. Ideally, I would one day like to pursue a career in game development, whether as part of a larger studio, or developing my own indie titles.

Flash in the Dark

Made using the Godot Engine in under 48 hours, this game was my entry for the Duck Sauce Jam 2024 by Duck Sauce Games, for the theme of "Discovery". This is a 2D puzzle-platformer, where you're trapped in a series of dark rooms. When you flash your light, you discover hidden clues to guide you towards the exit. Some clues will always appear, some only appear if you flash your light close to them. Remember the clues well, as your light needs to recharge, taking longer with each use. There are 5 rooms to escape. Play here

Flow Runner

Inspired by the theme "Flow", this game was my entry for the Duck Sauce Jam 2023 by Duck Sauce Games, made in under 48 hours using the Godot Engine. In this first-person 3D platformer, your goal is to survive as long as you can while your health slowly depletes. Standing still drains your health faster, while grabbing the golden orbs appearing around the arena replenishes it. Keeping an uninterrupted flow of movement is vital as you make your way from one orb to the next, to ensure you'll make it in time. Play here

Box Cluttering Chaos

Made in under 24 hours using the Godot Engine, this game was my entry for the Duck Sauce Jam 2022 by Duck Sauce Games, which had the theme of "Chaos". This is a simple 2D game, where you explode arrangements of boxes to try to get them as cluttered as possible. Your score is determined by the distance each box reaches from its starting point, with various bonuses to further boost your score. You have a limited number of explosions to use, so you need to position them strategically to maximise your score. Box arrangements are slightly randomized each time you play, to keep the game varied. Play here
